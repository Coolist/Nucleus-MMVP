Nucleus

Nucleus connects all the smart devices in your home and helps you create intelligent interactions between them.

Nucleus is an open system that allows you to connect many different devices to a simple, consistent interface. You can create connections between devices and manage them from anywhere.

Tasks

Easily create tasks that let your smart devices talk to each other. Create tasks such as turning off the lights when you’re away or lock the doors and turning down the thermostat when you go to bed.

Spaces

Organize and manage your smart devices by the room they are in and access them from anywhere. No matter how many devices you have, you’ll know what you’re interacting with.

History

Look back at the events within your home. Find out when your thermostat changed or when your front door was locked.
